About

Emergency medicine researcher, developer, and operations lead

Who I Am

I'm David Spatholt — Strategic Operations and Data Manager in the Department of Emergency Medicine at The Ohio State University Wexner Medical Center. I work at the intersection of health services research, data engineering, and software development, supporting a portfolio of NIH-funded research on pain, addiction, trauma recovery, and health equity.

What I Do

I run the research operations for the Population Health & Health Services (PH2S) program, including the HealthNow clinical program — ED-based screening, prevention, and linkage-to-care services. My work spans data architecture, grant logistics, compliance, and building the tools that make research faster and more reproducible.

I also build software. Dashboards, schedulers, watchfaces, simulations — if it solves a real problem and I can learn something building it, I'm interested. I use LLMs extensively as a development accelerator and collect the resulting mini apps in the gallery.
