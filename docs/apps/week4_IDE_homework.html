ASSIGNMENT 3: TIMELINE CALCULATION PRACTICE

Due: Friday 2pm | Time: 15 minutes

Instructions

Calculate UADE reporting deadlines, assuming no federal holidays unless stated. Use working day formula: count M-F only (exclude Sat, Sun).

Scenario 1: BASIC

UADE learned by investigator: Monday, March 18. Calculate: Investigator deadline to report to sponsor/IRB. | CFR: 21 CFR 812.150(a)(1)

Scenario 2: WEEKEND INTERRUPTION

Investigator learns of UADE Friday, April 25. Calculate: 10 working days forward (avoid weekend). | CFR: 21 CFR 812.150(a)(1)

Scenario 3: MULTI-SITE SPONSOR REPORTING

Sponsor receives investigator's UADE report Thursday, May 16. Calculate: Sponsor deadline to report to FDA, all IRBs, all investigators. | CFR: 21 CFR 812.150(b)(1)

Scenario 4: UNREASONABLE RISK DETERMINATION

Sponsor receives UADE notice: Monday, June 3. Sponsor evaluates and determines unreasonable risk: Wednesday, June 5. Calculate: LATEST termination deadline (5 working days). | CFR: 21 CFR 812.46(b)

Scenario 5: HOLIDAY COMPLICATION

UADE notice received: Friday, December 20. Federal holidays: Christmas (Dec 25-26), New Year's Day (Jan 1). Calculate: Sponsor's reporting deadline (10 working days). | CFR: 21 CFR 812.150(b)(1)

Scenario 6: INVESTIGATOR LATE REPORTING

Event occurs: Monday, March 11. Investigator doesn't report until: Friday, March 29. Question: Has investigator violated 812.150(a)(1)? When was deadline? | CFR: 21 CFR 812.150(a)(1)

Scenario 7: NSR→SR DISAGREEMENT

IRB notifies sponsor of disagreement: Friday, March 15. Calculate: Sponsor deadline to notify FDA (5 working days). | CFR: 21 CFR 812.150(b)(9)

Scenario 8: WITHDRAWAL OF APPROVAL

IRB sends notification: Tuesday, April 2. Calculate: Sponsor deadline to notify FDA, all other IRBs, all investigators (5 working days). | CFR: 21 CFR 812.150(b)(2)

Scenario 9: DEVICE RECALL/DISPOSITION

Request made: Monday, September 8. Calculate: Deadline to notify FDA, IRBs of recall/disposition request (5 working days). | CFR: 21 CFR 812.150(b)(6)

Scenario 10: INVESTIGATOR FINAL REPORT

IDE investigation completed: Friday, May 31. Calculate: Deadline for investigator to submit final report to sponsor, IRB (3 months). | CFR: 21 CFR 812.150(a)(6)
