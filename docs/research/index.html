Role

Clinical Research Operations

As Clinical Research Manager and Data Team Leader at Ohio State, I support a portfolio of NIH-funded research in the Department of Emergency Medicine. My work focuses on the operational backbone — budgeting, grant management, data systems, and the infrastructure that makes research possible.

Previously at the University of Cincinnati (EIP program), I led data operations for COVID testing and SUD/HIV screening research, deploying dashboards, redesigning data capture systems, and conducting R-based analyses.
