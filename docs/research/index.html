Research

Clinical research operations and scholarly contributions in emergency medicine and public health

Role

Clinical Research Operations

As Clinical Research Manager and Data Team Leader in the Department of Emergency Medicine at Ohio State, I support a portfolio of NIH-funded clinical research. My work focuses on the operational backbone — budgeting, grant management, data systems, and the infrastructure that makes research possible.

Previously at the University of Cincinnati, I led program operations for the Test & Protect (EIP) team, deployed dashboards for COVID testing operations, and conducted R-based analyses for SUD/HIV screening programs.
